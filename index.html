Devilled Cashew

Bold, fiery, and full of character — our Devilled Cashews are tossed in a spicy blend of Sri Lankan chili, aromatic spices, and a hint of sweetness. Each crunchy bite delivers a thrilling kick that keeps you reaching for more.

Oven-Baked Full Cashew

Pure, simple, and naturally delicious — these oven-baked whole cashews are roasted slowly to golden perfection. Lightly crisp on the outside, rich and buttery on the inside — this is cashew in its most wholesome, irresistible form.

Garlic & Salt Cashew

Savory goodness meets rich crunch. Infused with roasted garlic and just the right sprinkle of sea salt, this classic favorite balances bold flavor with the natural creaminess of premium Sri Lankan cashews.

Burnt Cashew

For the flavor adventurer — our Burnt Cashews offer a uniquely smoky, caramelized twist. Deep roasted to a bold finish, they deliver an intense aroma and rich, toasted depth that’s addictive in every handful.
